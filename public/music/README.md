# Wedding Music

Add your wedding music file here as `wedding-music.mp3`.

## Recommended Songs

### Classical (Most Popular for Weddings)
1. **Canon in D** by Pachelbel - The most iconic wedding piece
2. **Clair de Lune** by Debussy - Romantic and dreamy
3. **Air on the G String** by Bach - Sophisticated and elegant
4. **Gymnopédie No. 1** by Satie - Modern, minimalist
5. **Salut d'Amour** by Elgar - Sweet and tender

### Modern Instrumental
1. **A Thousand Years** by Christina Perri (instrumental version)
2. **All of Me** by John Legend (instrumental version)
3. **Perfect** by Ed Sheeran (instrumental version)

## How to Add Music

1. Download your chosen song as an MP3 file
2. Rename it to `wedding-music.mp3`
3. Place it in this directory (`/public/music/`)
4. The music player will automatically detect and play it

## Audio Settings

- **Volume**: Set to 25% (0.25) - comfortable background level
- **Loop**: Enabled - music will repeat continuously
- **Format**: MP3 (most compatible)
- **Recommended length**: 2-5 minutes (will loop seamlessly)

## Where to Find Music

- **YouTube Audio Library** (free, royalty-free)
- **Spotify** (download with premium)
- **iTunes/Apple Music**
- **Amazon Music**
- **Free Music Archive** (royalty-free)

## Tips

- Choose instrumental versions to avoid distracting lyrics
- Test the volume on different devices
- Ensure the file loops smoothly (fade in/out at ends)
- Keep file size under 10MB for faster loading
